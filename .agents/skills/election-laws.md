# Election Laws & Compliance — Knowledge Base

## Section 49P (Tendered Votes / Impersonation)
- **Law**: Section 49P of Conduct of Elections Rules, 1961
- **When**: Someone has already voted using your identity (voter impersonation)
- **Your Rights**: You can demand a "Challenged Vote" / Tendered Ballot
- **Process**: (1) Inform Presiding Officer immediately. (2) Fill Form 49AA. (3) Your vote is recorded on a tendered ballot paper (not EVM). (4) File FIR under IPC Section 171D (personation at election).
- **Helpline**: Call ECI 1950 to escalate

## Section 49(2) — Name Missing from Electoral Roll
- **Law**: Section 49(2) of the Representation of the People Act, 1951
- **When**: Your name is not found in the voter list at your polling booth
- **Process**: (1) Show EPIC card to Presiding Officer. (2) Request a Tendered Vote under Sec 49(2). (3) Fill Form 49AA. (4) File complaint at DEO office within 7 days.

## NOTA (None of the Above)
- **Legal Basis**: Rule 49-O, affirmed by Supreme Court Judgment (NOTA, 2013)
- **What It Is**: The last button on the EVM, allows you to reject all candidates
- **Effect**: NOTA votes are counted and reported officially but currently do not affect the result (candidate with most votes wins regardless)
- **Privacy**: Your NOTA vote is completely secret

## Form 6 — New Voter Registration
- **Who**: First-time voters aged 18+ on qualifying date (January 1st)
- **Documents Required**: Proof of Age (Birth Certificate / Class 10 Marksheet), Proof of Address (Aadhaar / Passport / Utility Bill)
- **Where to Apply**: Online at voters.eci.gov.in or at local ERO office
- **Timeline**: Apply at least 30 days before election for guaranteed inclusion

## Form 8A — Address Transfer
- **Who**: Registered voters who have relocated to a different constituency
- **Process**: File Form 8A online or at new constituency's ERO office
- **Documents**: New address proof required
- **Important**: Old registration is automatically deleted once transfer is approved

## Booth Level Officer (BLO)
- **Role**: Door-to-door verification officer assigned to every polling booth area
- **Contact**: Find your BLO at voters.eci.gov.in or call 1950
- **When**: BLO visits after Form 6 submission to verify identity and address

## Polling Day Rules
- **Hours**: Polling typically 7:00 AM to 6:00 PM (varies by state)
- **ID Required**: EPIC card or any of 12 approved photo IDs (Aadhaar, Passport, DL, PAN, etc.)
- **Prohibited**: Mobile phones inside polling booth, campaigning within 100m of booth
- **Employee Rights**: Paid leave must be granted for voting under Section 135B

## ECI Emergency Helpline
- **Number**: 1950 (24×7 on polling day)
- **Also**: cVIGIL app for reporting violations with photo/video evidence
